Core Rules

Roster Rules

  • 8 starters total.
  • No kickers.
  • At least 1 starter from each active NFL team.
  • If all 4 teams are active, max 3 from one NFL team.

Bye Week Rules

  • If a team is on bye, use remaining active teams only.
  • Playoffs require at least 1 from each remaining active team.
