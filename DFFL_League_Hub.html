Core Rules

Roster Rules

  • 8 starters total.
  • No kickers.
  • Must start at least 1 player from each active NFL team in your division.
  • If all 4 teams are active, max 3 starters from any single NFL team.

Bye Week Rules

  • If one team is on bye, use remaining active teams only.
  • During playoffs, minimum 1 starter from each remaining active team.
  • No delayed scoring from future weeks.

Tiebreakers

  1. Head-to-head record
  2. Total points scored
  3. Highest single-week score
  4. Coin flip
